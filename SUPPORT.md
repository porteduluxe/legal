# Paniero — Support

**Paniero** is a shopping list app by Porte Du Luxe LLC. This page is the official support resource for the Paniero **iOS** and **Android** apps.

## Contact us

The fastest way to reach us is by email:

**[info@porteduluxe.com](mailto:info@porteduluxe.com)**

We answer support emails personally, usually within two business days.

When you write in, it helps to include:

- Which version you're running (Paniero → menu → **About**)
- Your device model and OS version (e.g. *iPhone 15 Pro, iOS 18.4* or *Pixel 8, Android 15*)
- What you were trying to do and what went wrong, with a screenshot if you can

## Common questions

### I forgot my password.

On the login screen, tap **Forgot password?**, enter your email, and we'll send a reset link from Firebase. For security reasons the confirmation message is the same whether or not an account exists for that address — check your inbox (and spam folder) for the reset email.

### How do I share a list with someone else?

Open the store you want to share, tap the menu in the top-right, and choose **Share List**. Enter their email address. They need to have signed in to Paniero at least once for the invite to find them. Either of you can leave a shared list at any time from the same screen.

### How do I delete my account and everything in it?

Open the menu in the top-right of the Stores screen and choose **Delete Account**. This permanently removes every store, item, and photo you own from our database, removes you from any lists that were shared with you, and deletes your sign-in record. The action is immediate and cannot be reversed.

If for any reason you can't use the in-app option, email us and we'll handle it for you within 30 days.

### Voice input isn't transcribing what I say.

On iOS, voice input uses Apple's Speech Recognition framework. The first time you tap the microphone, you'll be asked to grant Microphone and Speech Recognition permission — both are required. If you accidentally denied either, you can re-enable them in Settings → Paniero.

On Android, the microphone hands off to your device's built-in speech-recognition service (usually Google's). Some devices require an active internet connection for recognition to work.

### A photo I attached looks blurry.

Paniero scales photos down to a maximum dimension of 1200 pixels to keep your lists fast to sync. If you need to keep the original, store it in your camera roll separately.
